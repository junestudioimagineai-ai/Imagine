Understanding Pricing

Why costs vary and how we ensure fair pricing

Faster delivery requires more sophisticated infrastructure and priority routing through our provider network. Basic tiers deliver within 15-60 minutes, while Premium and Elite tiers offer instant or scheduled delivery with higher success rates.

Higher retention accounts (real-looking profiles with activity history) cost more to maintain. Our Elite tier includes guaranteed refills if drops occur within 30 days, ensuring your investment is protected.

Each social platform has different detection mechanisms. Instagram and TikTok require more sophisticated delivery patterns to avoid flags, while Twitter and Facebook allow faster throughput. Our system automatically optimizes delivery per platform.

Ordering 1,000+ units unlocks 10% discount, 5,000+ gets 20%, and 10,000+ receives 30% off. Bundle packages combining multiple services or platforms receive additional 20-50% discounts automatically applied at checkout.
